I am honored to share that I have been awarded the **SCIC Scholarship — "Nâng bước tài năng trẻ" 2026** by the State Capital Investment Corporation (SCIC).

This scholarship recognizes young Vietnamese talent and supports their academic and professional development. It is a meaningful recognition of the research work I have been pursuing at the Business AI Lab, NEU.

## About the Scholarship

The SCIC "Nâng bước tài năng trẻ" (Nurturing Young Talent) scholarship program is awarded by the State Capital Investment Corporation to outstanding young Vietnamese students showing promise in science, technology, and innovation. The program aims to support the next generation of Vietnamese researchers and professionals.

## What This Means

Receiving this scholarship is a great encouragement to keep pushing forward in my research on AI for healthcare and fraud detection. The recognition from SCIC motivates me to continue contributing to impactful work in AI and to represent Vietnamese talent in the international research community.

I am grateful to my supervisors at Business AI Lab, my family, and everyone who has supported my journey so far.
