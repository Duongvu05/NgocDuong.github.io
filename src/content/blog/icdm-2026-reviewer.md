I am honored to be serving as a **reviewer for ICDM 2026** — the IEEE International Conference on Data Mining. ICDM is ranked **CORE A\*** and **CCF A**, making it one of the most prestigious venues in data mining and knowledge discovery research.

Peer review is a cornerstone of the scientific community, and it is a privilege to contribute to the quality and integrity of work presented at ICDM. I look forward to providing constructive, rigorous feedback to help advance the field.

Being part of the ICDM program committee as an early-career researcher is a meaningful milestone, and I hope to give back to the community in the same spirit that reviewers and mentors have supported my own work.
